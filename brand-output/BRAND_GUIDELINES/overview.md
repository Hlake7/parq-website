# Parq AI Brand Guidelines — Overview

**Version:** 1.0
**Date:** February 19, 2026

---

## Brand at a Glance

**Parq AI** is an autonomous parking compliance platform that uses computer vision to monitor, enforce, and report on parking rules 24/7. We serve property managers, municipalities, and commercial real estate operators.

| Element | Summary |
|---|---|
| **Archetype** | The Sentinel — vigilant, precise, always-on |
| **Tagline** | Every spot. Every hour. Every time. |
| **Primary Color** | Parq Navy `#0A1628` |
| **Accent Color** | Parq Teal `#00C2B7` |
| **Headline Font** | General Sans (geometric sans-serif) |
| **Body Font** | Inter (neo-grotesque sans-serif) |
| **Data Font** | JetBrains Mono (monospaced) |
| **Logo** | Coordinate Grid (Diamond Variant) — 3x3 grid rotated 45° with teal detection node |
| **Preferred Background** | Dark (Parq Navy `#0A1628`) |

---

## Brand Personality

**Confident and direct** — speaks with quiet authority, not hype. Professional but approachable. Explains AI in human terms. Leads with outcomes and measurable results.

### Personality Spectrum

- More formal than casual (0.65)
- More serious than playful (0.75)
- Balanced between technical and simple (0.55)
- More restrained than bold (0.45)

---

## Core Values

1. **Precision** — We measure everything and guess nothing. 99.2% accuracy is our baseline, not our ceiling.
2. **Autonomy** — Parking compliance should run itself. Set it, trust it, verify it.
3. **Transparency** — Clear communication. Explainable AI. Client-owned data.
4. **Partnership** — We succeed when our clients succeed. Technology partner, not replacement operator.

---

## Messaging Pillars

1. **Autonomous Compliance** — Your parking lot monitors itself.
2. **Revenue Intelligence** — See what your parking is really worth.
3. **Effortless Deployment** — Go live in weeks, not months.
4. **Your Data, Your Control** — Full transparency. Full ownership.
