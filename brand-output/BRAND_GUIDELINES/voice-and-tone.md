# Parq AI Brand Guidelines — Voice and Tone

---

## Brand Voice

**Confident and direct.** Parq AI speaks with quiet authority, not hype. Professional but approachable. We explain AI in human terms and lead with outcomes rather than technology.

## Writing Principles

### Do

- **Lead with outcomes and measurable results.** "35% average revenue increase" before "computer vision detection pipeline."
- **Use precise numbers over vague superlatives.** "99.2% accuracy" instead of "industry-leading performance."
- **Speak to business problems first, technology second.** Start with the pain point, then introduce the solution.
- **Use active, present-tense language.** "Parq detects violations in real time" not "Violations can be detected."
- **Be concise.** Every sentence should earn its place. If a paragraph can be a sentence, make it a sentence.

### Do Not

- **Use fear-based enforcement language.** Never "catching violators" or "cracking down." We are compliance partners, not enforcers.
- **Over-promise with buzzwords.** Avoid "revolutionary," "disruptive," "game-changing," "next-generation."
- **Sound like a surveillance company.** Frame as compliance and optimization, not monitoring and control.
- **Talk down to non-technical buyers.** No dense AI terminology without explanation. If you must use a technical term, define it immediately.
- **Use passive voice or hedging.** Not "might" or "could potentially" — state what the product does.

## Audience-Specific Tone Adjustments

### Property Managers
- **Tone:** Practical, empathetic, solution-oriented
- **Focus:** Eliminate frustration. Reduce workload. Show them what they can stop doing.
- **Example:** "Stop chasing parking violators. Parq monitors your lots 24/7 so your team can focus on what actually grows property value."

### Municipal Planners
- **Tone:** Data-driven, policy-aware, measured
- **Focus:** Evidence-based decisions. Staffing efficiency. Public accountability.
- **Example:** "Real-time occupancy data across every district. Make zoning and rate decisions based on what's actually happening in your lots — not last quarter's spreadsheet."

### Asset Owners / Investors
- **Tone:** ROI-focused, portfolio-level, strategic
- **Focus:** NOI contribution. Portfolio visibility. Scalable standardization.
- **Example:** "Parking is the most under-analyzed asset in your portfolio. Parq gives you property-level performance data across every lot you own."

## Messaging Pillars

### 1. Autonomous Compliance
**Core message:** Your parking lot monitors itself.

- Proof points: 99.2% detection accuracy, 24/7 monitoring, 90% reduction in manual enforcement
- Voice: Confident, relief-oriented ("stop doing X")

### 2. Revenue Intelligence
**Core message:** See what your parking is really worth.

- Proof points: 35% average revenue increase, live dashboards, data-driven pricing
- Voice: Analytical, revealing ("hidden patterns," "what's actually happening")

### 3. Effortless Deployment
**Core message:** Go live in weeks, not months.

- Proof points: Existing camera infrastructure, weeks to deploy, no long-term contracts
- Voice: Reassuring, practical ("no rip-and-replace," "layer it onto what you have")

### 4. Your Data, Your Control
**Core message:** Full transparency. Full ownership.

- Proof points: Client-owned data, explainable AI, open integrations
- Voice: Trustworthy, partnership-oriented ("you stay in charge")

## Naming Convention

- **Brand name:** Parq AI (always with the space, always with capitalized AI)
- **In the logo:** "Parq" in General Sans, "AI" in JetBrains Mono, teal color
- **In running text:** "Parq AI" on first reference, "Parq" acceptable on subsequent references
- **Never:** "ParqAI" (no space), "PARQ" (all caps), "parq" (lowercase)

## Competitive Language

### vs AI Competitors (Metropolis, Reef)
**Positioning line:** "They want to own your parking. We want to optimize it."
- Emphasize: property-owner aligned, data ownership, asset-light model
- Never: attack competitors by name in public-facing content

### vs Traditional Operators (LAZ, SP+, ABM)
**Positioning line:** "The accuracy of AI. The accountability your property deserves."
- Emphasize: 24/7 coverage, consistency, 90% lower labor cost, rapid deployment
- Never: position traditional operators as incompetent — frame as an evolution, not a replacement of people
